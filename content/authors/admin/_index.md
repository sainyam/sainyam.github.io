I am an assistant professor in Computer Science at {{< staticref "https://www.cs.cornell.edu/" "newtab" >}}Cornell University{{< /staticref >}}. Before that, I was a {{< staticref "https://cifellows2021.org/2021-class/" "newtab" >}}Computing Innovation Fellow{{< /staticref >}} pursuing postdoctoral research at the University of Chicago. My research develops data science tools that make analytics more effective, reliable, and responsible.<br>

My work draws on data management, causal inference, machine learning, theoretical computer science, and HCI to build trustworthy systems with an emphasis on robustness, explainability, and fairness. I received my Ph.D. from the University of Massachusetts Amherst and my undergraduate degree from the Indian Institute of Technology Delhi. Prior to joining UMass, I worked at Xerox Research Centre India, Bangalore.
